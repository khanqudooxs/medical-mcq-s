1. **Who proposed the Cell Theory?**
   A) Robert Hooke
   B) Schleiden and Schwann
   C) Louis Pasteur
   D) Charles Darwin
   *Answer: B (Explanation: In 1838-39, they stated that all living things are composed of cells.)*

2. **Who discovered the first cell?**
   A) Galileo Galilei
   B) Robert Hooke
   C) Antonie van Leeuwenhoek
   D) Isaac Newton
