# Topic 18: Biodiversity and Classification (300 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Taxonomy, Kingdoms & General Diversity

1. **What is the primary definition of 'Biodiversity'?**
   A) The selective breeding of domesticated animals
   B) The variety and variability of life on Earth (including species, genes, and ecosystems)
   C) The total number of human beings currently living on the planet
   D) The different color variations found in ornamental plants
   *Answer: B (Explanation: It encompasses the entire spectrum of living organisms).*

2. **What is the process of dividing organisms into hierarchical groups and sub-groups called?**
   A) Natural Selection
   B) Biological Classification
   C) Biological Evolution
   D) Geographical Distribution
   *Answer: B*

3. **'Taxonomy' is the branch of biology that specifically deals with ______ .**
   A) The exclusive study of prehistoric fossils
   B) The naming, defining, and systematic classification of organisms
   C) The search for potential life in outer space
   D) The chemical composition of different soil types
   *Answer: B*

4. **How many Kingdoms are organisms divided into in the modern biological system?**
   A) 2
   B) 5 (Monera, Protista, Fungi, Plantae, Animalia)
   C) 10
   D) 3
   *Answer: B (This system was famously proposed by Robert Whittaker).*

5. **Who historically proposed the Five Kingdom system of classification?**
   A) Carolus Linnaeus
   B) Robert Whittaker (in 1969)
   C) Aristotle
   D) Charles Darwin
   *Answer: B*

6. **What type of organisms are primarily categorized within Kingdom Monera?**
   A) Multicellular green plants
   B) Prokaryotic unicellular organisms (e.g., Bacteria)
   C) Saprophytic fungi
   D) Highly complex human beings
   *Answer: B*

7. **A defining characteristic of Prokaryotes is that they lack ______ .**
   A) Genomic DNA

   C) A protective cell wall
   D) Functional ribosomes
   *Answer: B*

8. **Kingdom Protista generally includes which types of organisms?**
