# Topic 16: Biotechnology (300 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Genetic Engineering, DNA Technology & Applications

1. **What is the fundamental definition of Biotechnology?**
   A) The study of ancient and prehistoric life
   B) The use of living organisms or their products for the benefit of humans
   C) The scientific study of outer space
   ) The study of geological rock formations
   *Answer: B (Explanation: This field includes genetic engineering, fermentation, and various industrial processes).*

2. **In 'Genetic Engineering', DNA is primarily ______ .**
   A) Completely eliminated from the cell
   B) Manipulated, altered, or transferred between organisms
   C) Colored for aesthetic purposes only
   D) Observed without any physical intervention
   *Answer: B (Note: This is also referred to as Recombinant DNA Technology).*

3. **What are the enzymes that function as 'molecular scissors' to cut DNA at specific sites?**
   A) Ligases
   B) Restriction Endonucleases
   C) Polymerases
   D) Lipases
   *Answer: B*

4. **From which organisms were 'Restriction Enzymes' first isolated?**
   A) Human cells
   B) Bacteria
   C) Viruses
   D) Higher plant species
   *Answer: B (Bacteria naturally use these enzymes as a defense mechanism against invading bacteriophages).*

5. **Which enzyme acts as 'molecular glue' to join DNA fragments together?**
   A) Restriction Enzyme
   B) DNA Ligase
   C) Helicase
   D) Primase
   *Answer: B*

6. **What is 'Recombinant DNA' (rDNA)?**
   A) Naturally occurring, unaltered DNA
   B) DNA molecule containing genes derived from two or more different sources or species
   C) Fragmented or broken DNA strands

   *Answer: B*
