## Part 1: Bacteria, Viruses, and Fungi

1. **How is 'Microbiology' defined?**
   A) The study of higher plant systems
   B) The study of microscopic organisms (including Bacteria, Viruses, Fungi, and Protists)
   C) The study of celestial bodies and deep space
   D) The study of large, multicellular vertebrate animals
   *Answer: B*

   C) Kingdom Fungi
