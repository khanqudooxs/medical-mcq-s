# Topic 08: Waves & Sound (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Nature and Types of Waves

1. **A disturbance that travels through space or matter carrying energy without transporting matter is a:**
   A) Particle
   B) Wave
   C) Force
   D) Vacuum
   *Answer: B*

2. **Waves that require a physical medium (like air, water, or string) to travel are:**
   A) Electromagnetic waves
   B) Mechanical Waves
   C) Matter waves
   D) Stationary waves
   *Answer: B*

3. **Waves that can travel through a vacuum (like light) are:**
   A) Mechanical waves
   B) Electromagnetic Waves
   C) Sound waves
   D) Seismic waves
   *Answer: B*

4. **In which type of wave do particles of the medium vibrate 'perpendicular' to the direction of wave travel?**
   A) Longitudinal
   B) Transverse Waves
   C) Sound
   D) Pressure waves
   *Answer: B (e.g., light, ripples on water).*

5. **In which type of wave do particles vibrate 'parallel' to the direction of wave travel?**
   A) Transverse
   B) Longitudinal Waves
   C) Radio waves
   D) Light waves
   *Answer: B (e.g., sound waves).*

6. **The highest point on a transverse wave is called the:**
   A) Trough
   B) Crest
   C) Node
   D) Amplitude
   *Answer: B*

7. **The lowest point on a transverse wave is called the:**
   A) Crest
   B) Trough
   C) Anti-node
   D) Base
   *Answer: B*

8. **In longitudinal waves, regions where particles are crowded together are:**
   A) Rarefactions
   B) Compressions
   C) Crests
   D) Nodes
   *Answer: B*

9. **In longitudinal waves, regions where particles are spread apart are:**
   A) Compressions
   B) Rarefactions
   C) Troughs
   D) Crests
   *Answer: B*

10. **A single disturbance that moves through a medium is a:**
    A) Periodic wave
    B) Wave Pulse
    C) Frequency
    D) Harmonic
    *Answer: B*
