# Topic 06: Properties of Matter (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Elasticity and Hooke's Law

1. **The property of a body to regain its original shape and size after the removal of deforming force is:**
   A) Plasticity
   B) Elasticity
   C) Viscosity
   D) Brittleness
   *Answer: B*

2. **A material that does not regain its original shape and remains deformed is said to be:**
   A) Elastic
   B) Plastic
   C) Rigid
   D) Fluid
   *Answer: B*

3. **Force applied per unit area is called:**
   A) Strain
   B) Stress ($\sigma$)
   C) Pressure
   D) Both B and C (Pressure is used for fluids)
   *Answer: D*

4. **The fractional change in dimensions (length, volume, or shape) of a body is:**
   A) Stress
   B) Strain ($\epsilon$)
   C) Friction
   D) Momentum
   *Answer: B*

5. **Which of the following has NO units?**
   A) Stress
   B) Strain (it's a ratio of same quantities)
   C) Pressure
   D) Young's Modulus
   *Answer: B*

6. **Hooke's Law states that within the elastic limit, stress is ______ proportional to strain.**
   A) Inversely
   B) Directly ($\text{Stress} \propto \text{Strain}$)
   C) Not
   *Answer: B*

7. **The ratio of longitudinal stress to longitudinal strain is:**
   A) Bulk Modulus
   B) Young's Modulus ($Y$)
   C) Shear Modulus
   D) Friction coefficient
   *Answer: B*

8. **The unit of Young's Modulus in SI is:**
   A) $N \cdot m$
   B) $N/m^2$ (or Pascal, Pa)
   C) $Joule$
   D) $kg/m^3$
   *Answer: B*

9. **Which is more elastic: Steel or Rubber?**
   A) Rubber
   B) Steel (it requires more stress for the same strain)
   *Answer: B*

10. **The maximum stress a body can withstand without permanent deformation is its:**
    A) Breaking point
    ) Elastic Limit
    C) Plastic limit
    *Answer: B*

11. **Ratio of volume stress to volume strain is the:**
    A) Young's Modulus
    B) Bulk Modulus ($B$)
    C) Shear Modulus
    *Answer: B*

12. **The reciprocal of Bulk Modulus is called:**
    A) Elasticity
    B) Compressibility
    C) Density
    D) Viscosity
    *Answer: B*

13. **Substances that undergo large plastic deformation before breaking (like copper) are:**
    A) Brittle
    B) Ductile
    C) Amorphous
    *Answer: B*

14. **Substances that break just after the elastic limit (like glass) are:**
    A) Ductile
    B) Brittle
    C) Elastic
    *Answer: B*

15. **Energy stored in a deformed body is ______ energy.**
    A) Kinetic
    B) Elastic Potential
    C) Thermal
    *Answer: B*

## Part 3: Pascal's, Archimedes' and Bernoulli's Principles

31. **'Pressure applied to an enclosed fluid is transmitted undiminished to every portion of the fluid' is:**
    A) Archimedes' Principle
    B) Pascal's Law
    C) Bernoulli's Principle
    D) Boyle's Law
    *Answer: B*

32. **Hydraulic brakes and hydraulic lifts work on the principle of:**
    A) Momentum
    B) Pascal's Law
    C) Friction
    D) Heat
    *Answer: B*

33. **The upward force exerted by a fluid on an object immersed in it is:**
    A) Friction
    B) Upthrust (or Buoyant Force)
    C) Weight
    D) Tension
    *Answer: B*

34. **'Upthrust is equal to the weight of the fluid displaced by the object' is:**
    A) Pascal's Law
    B) Archimedes' Principle
    C) Newton's Law
    D) Dalton's Law
    *Answer: B*

35. **An object will float if its density is ______ than the density of the fluid.**
    A) Greater
    B) Lesser
    C) Equal
    D) Both B and C
    *Answer: D*

36. **An object will sink if its density is ______ than the density of the fluid.**
    A) Lesser
    B) Greater
    C) Equal
    *Answer: B*

37. **A ship made of steel floats on water because its average density (including air inside) is ______ than water.**
    A) Greater
    B) Lesser
    C) Infinite
    *Answer: B*

38. **The instrument used to measure the density/purity of milk is:**
    A) Hydrometer
    B) Lactometer

39. **The instrument used to measure the density of liquids is a:**
    A) Barometer
    B) Hydrometer
    C) Speedometer
    *Answer: B*

40. **For a steady, incompressible flow, as velocity increases, the pressure in the fluid ______ .**
    A) Increases
    B) Decreases (Bernoulli's Principle)
    C) Stays same
    *Answer: B*

41. **Bernoulli's Principle is a statement of the Law of Conservation of ______ for fluids.**
    A) Mass
    B) Energy
    C) Momentum
    *Answer: B*

42. **The lift of an airplane wing is explained by:**
    A) Pascal's Law
    B) Bernoulli's Principle (Air moves faster over the top, creating low pressure)
    C) Archimedes' Principle
    *Answer: B*

43. **A 'Venturi meter' is used to measure the ______ of a fluid.**
    A) Density
    B) Speed of flow
    C) Color
    *Answer: B*

44. **The swinging (curving) of a spinning cricket ball in air is due to:**
    A) Friction only
    B) Bernoulli's effect (Magnus effect)
    C) Gravity
    *Answer: B*

45. **Bunsen burner works on the principle of ______ .**
    A) Archimedes
    B) Bernoulli (Gas jet creates low pressure to draw in air)
    C) Pascal
    *Answer: B*

## Part 4: Viscosity and Surface Tension

    A) Surface tension
    B) Viscosity ($\eta$)
    C) Pressure
    D) Density
    *Answer: B*

47. **A fluid that flows easily (like water) has ______ viscosity.**
    A) High
    B) Low
    C) Zero
    *Answer: B*

    A) Low
    B) High

    *Answer: B*

49. **Viscosity of liquids ______ as temperature increases.**
    A) Increases
    B) Decreases (they become thinner)
    C) Stays same
    *Answer: B*

50. **Viscosity of gases ______ as temperature increases.**
    A) Decreases
    B) Increases
    C) Stays same
    *Answer: B*

51. **The SI unit of viscosity is:**
    A) Pascal
    B) $Pa \cdot s$ (Pascal-second) or $N \cdot s/m^2$
    C) Poise (CGS unit)
    D) Both B and C are related
    *Answer: B*

52. **A body falling through a viscous fluid eventually reaches a constant speed called:**
    A) Escape velocity
    B) Terminal Velocity
    C) Orbital velocity
    D) Critical velocity
    *Answer: B*

53. **Stokes' Law defines the viscous drag on a spherical object as $F = 6\pi \eta r v$.**

    B) False

54. **The property of a liquid surface to act like a stretched elastic membrane is:**
    A) Viscosity
    B) Surface Tension ($\gamma$)

55. **Surface tension is caused by ______ forces between liquid molecules.**
    A) Adhesive

    A) Gravity

57. **The SI unit of surface tension is:**
    A) $N/m^2$

60. **Surface tension of a liquid ______ as temperature increases.**
    A) Increases
    B) Decreases

    A) Adhesion
    B) Cohesion

## Part 5: Fluids in Motion and Blood Flow

66. **A flow where every particle of fluid follows the same path as the previous one is:**
    A) Turbulent
    B) Streamline (or Laminar) Flow
    C) Random
    D) Circular
    *Answer: B*

67. **A flow characterized by irregular fluctuations and mixing is:**
    A) Streamline
    B) Turbulent Flow
    C) Ideal

68. **The speed beyond which a streamline flow becomes turbulent is the:**
    A) Escape velocity
    B) Critical Velocity
    C) Terminal velocity
    *Answer: B*

    A) Solid, Liquid

    C) Rest, Motion
    *Answer: B*

    B) False

71. **According to the Equation of Continuity, when a pipe narrows, the fluid speed ______ .**

    B) Increases

    A) Turbulent
    B) Laminar (Streamline)
    C) Pulsating

    *Answer: D*

73. **When an artery narrows, the blood speed ______ and the lateral pressure on the walls ______ .**

    *Answer: B*

74. **This pressure drop in narrowed arteries can lead to cardiovascular issues.**

75. **Sphygmomanometer is used to measure ______ pressure.**
