# Topic 18: Environmental Physics & Energy (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Sources of Energy

1. **Energy that can be replenished naturally over a short period is:**
   A) Non-renewable
   B) Renewable Energy

   A) Rocks and Water

   C) Stars

5. **Which fossil fuel is often found with petroleum and is used for heating?**

   B) Natural Gas (Methane)

   A) Oxygen

   A) The Moon
