# Topic 19: Communication Physics (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Waves and Signals

1. **The transmission of information from one place to another is:**
   A) Transport
   B) Communication
   C) Navigation
   D) Storage
   *Answer: B*

2. **Every communication system consists of a Transmitter, a Channel, and a:**
   A) Battery
   B) Receiver
   C) Screen
   D) Radio
   *Answer: B*

3. **In communication, 'Information' is converted into a/an ______ for transmission.**
   A) Mass
   B) Signal (Electric or Electromagnetic)
   C) Sound only
   *Answer: B*

4. **Signals that vary continuously with time (like human voice) are:**
   A) Digital
   B) Analog Signals
   C) Binary
   *Answer: B*

5. **Signals that have discrete values (0 and 1) are:**
   A) Analog
   B) Digital Signals
   C) Waveform
   *Answer: B*

6. **The number of cycles per second of a signal is its:**
   A) Amplitude
   B) Frequency ($f$)
   C) Wavelength
   *Answer: B*

7. **The unit of frequency is:**
   A) Watt
   B) Hertz (Hz)
   C) Volt
   D) Meter
   *Answer: B*

8. **The range of frequencies contained in a signal is its:**
   A) Channel
   B) Bandwidth
   C) Length
   *Answer: B*

9. **The height/strength of a wave is its:**
   A) Frequency
   B) Amplitude
   C) Phase
   *Answer: B*

10. **The loss of signal strength as it travels through a medium is:**
    A) Amplification
    B) Attenuation
    C) Distortion
    *Answer: B*

11. **Random unwanted signals that interfere with the original signal are called:**
    A) Music
    B) Noise
    C) Echo
    *Answer: B*

## Part 2: Modulation and Transmission

12. **Most low-frequency signals cannot be sent directly over long distances because they require very long ______ .**
    A) Wires
    B) Antennas
    C) Batteries
    *Answer: B*

13. **The process of overlaying a low-frequency signal on a high-frequency 'carrier' wave is:**
    A) Rectification
    B) Modulation
    C) Detection
    *Answer: B*

14. **AM stands for ______ Modulation.**
    A) Altitude
    B) Amplitude
    C) Accurate
    *Answer: B (Amplitude of carrier is changed).*

15. **FM stands for ______ Modulation.**
    A) Fast
    B) Frequency
    C) Functional
    *Answer: B (Frequency of carrier is changed).*

16. **Which provides better signal quality and less noise interference?**
    A) AM
    B) FM
    *Answer: B (frequency is more immune to noise than amplitude).*

17. **A device that recovers the original signal from a modulated carrier wave is a:**
    A) Modulator
    B) Demodulator (Detector)
    C) Battery
    *Answer: B*

18. **A 'Modem' (in computers) is a device that performs both ...... and ...... .**
    A) Speeding and Slowing
    B) Modulation and Demodulation
    C) Printing and Scanning
    *Answer: B*

19. **Wait, a 'Transducer' is a device that converts ****** into ****** .**
    A) Heat into light
    B) One form of energy into another (e.g., sound to electricity)
    *Answer: B (e.g., a microphone).*
