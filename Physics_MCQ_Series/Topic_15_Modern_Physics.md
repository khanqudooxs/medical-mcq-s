# Topic 15: Modern Physics & Relativity (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Introduction to Relativity

1. **'Classical Physics' (Newtonian) is highly accurate for objects moving at ______ speeds.**
   A) Light
   B) Low (compared to light)
   C) Supersonic
   D) Negative
   *Answer: B*

2. **'Modern Physics' is primarily concerned with phenomena occurring at ______ scales and ______ speeds.**
   A) Large, Zero
   B) Small (Atomic), High (Relativistic)
   C) Human, Walk
   *Answer: B*

3. **Who proposed the Special Theory of Relativity in 1905?**
   A) Newton
   B) Albert Einstein
   C) Hawking
   D) Maxwell
   *Answer: B*

4. **The first postulate of Special Relativity states that the laws of physics are the same in all ______ frames of reference.**
   A) Accelerating
   B) Inertial (non-accelerating)
   C) Rotating
   *Answer: B*

5. **The second postulate states that the speed of light ($c$) in a vacuum is ______ for all observers.**
   A) Different
   B) Constant ($3 \times 10^8 m/s$)
   C) Zero
   *Answer: B (even if the observer is moving).*

6. **The phenomenon where time slows down for an object moving at very high speed is:**
   A) Time expansion
   B) Time Dilation
   C) Time stop
   *Answer: B*

7. **The phenomenon where the length of an object appears shorter in the direction of motion at high speed is:**
   A) Expansion
   B) Length Contraction (Lorentz contraction)
   C) Shrinkage
   *Answer: B*

8. **As the speed of an object approaches the speed of light, its relativistic mass ______ .**
   A) Decreases
   B) Increases (approaching infinity at $v=c$)
   C) Stays same
   *Answer: B*

9. **Can an object with mass ($m > 0$) reach the speed of light?**
   A) Yes, with enough fuel
   B) No (it would require infinite energy)
   *Answer: B*

10. **The famous mass-energy equivalence equation is:**
    A) $F = ma$
    B) $E = mc^2$
    C) $PV = nRT$
    *Answer: B*

11. **In $E = mc^2$, small mass represents a ______ amount of energy.**
    A) Small
    B) Large (because $c^2$ is a very large number)
    C) Zero
    *Answer: B*

12. **The 'Twin Paradox' is a famous illustration of ______ .**
    A) Gravity
    B) Time Dilation
    C) Sound waves
    *Answer: B (Traveling twin returns younger than the one on Earth).*

13. **Einstein's General Theory of Relativity (1915) describes ______ as a curvature of space-time.**
    A) Magnetism
    B) Gravity
    C) Heat
    *Answer: B*

14. **Light from distant stars bends as it passes near the Sun. This is called ______ .**
    A) Refraction
    B) Gravitational Lensing
    C) Reflection
    *Answer: B*

15. **Black holes are regions where ______ is so strong that even light cannot escape.**
    A) Pressure
    B) Gravity
    C) Magnetism
    *Answer: B*
