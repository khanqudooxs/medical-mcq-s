# Topic 03: Dynamics - Laws of Motion (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Newton's First Law and Inertia

1. **'Dynamics' is the branch of mechanics that deals with:**
   A) Description of motion only
   B) Study of motion and the forces that cause it
   C) Fluids at rest
   D) Study of light
   *Answer: B*

2. **The property of a body to resist any change in its state of rest or uniform motion is called:**
   A) Friction
   B) Momentum
   C) Inertia
   D) Weight
   *Answer: C*

3. **Newton's First Law of Motion is also known as the Law of:**
   A) Acceleration
   B) Inertia
   C) Action and Reaction
   D) Conservation of energy
   *Answer: B*

4. **Inertia of a body depends directly on its:**
   A) Velocity
   B) Mass
   C) Shape
   D) Volume
   *Answer: B*

5. **Which object has more inertia: a bicycle or a heavy truck?**
   A) Bicycle
   B) Truck (due to greater mass)
   C) Both have same
   D) Depends on their speed
   *Answer: B*

6. **Newton's First Law states that if no net force acts on a body, it will:**
   A) Stop moving
   B) Continue in its state of rest or uniform motion in a straight line
   C) Move in a circle
   D) Accelerate
   *Answer: B*

7. **A passenger in a moving bus falls forward when the bus suddenly stops. This is due to:**
   A) Gravity
   B) Inertia of motion
   C) Friction
   D) Momentum
   *Answer: B (The lower body stops with the bus, but the upper body continues moving).*

8. **A passenger falls backward when a bus suddenly starts moving. This is due to:**
   A) Inertia of rest
   B) Friction
   C) Air pressure
   D) Weight
   *Answer: A*

9. **When you shake a branch of a tree, the leaves fall off due to:**
   A) Gravity only
   B) Inertia of rest of the leaves
   C) Momentum
   D) Magnetic force
   *Answer: B*

10. **A dust particle stays behind when a carpet is beaten with a stick. This is an example of:**
    A) First Law of Motion (Inertia)
    B) Second Law
    C) Third Law
    D) Law of gravitation
    *Answer: A*

54. **The maximum friction force that must be overcome to start a body moving is:**
    A) Kinetic friction
    B) Limiting Static Friction ($f_s$)
    C) Rolling friction
    D) Fluid friction
    *Answer: B*

55. **Friction force that acts when a body is sliding over another is:**
    A) Static friction
    B) Kinetic (or Sliding) Friction ($f_k$)
    C) Rolling friction
    D) Air resistance
    *Answer: B*

56. **Which is generally greater for the same pair of surfaces?**
    A) Kinetic friction
    B) Static friction

57. **Friction that acts when a body rolls over another surface is:**
    A) Sliding friction
    B) Rolling Friction
    C) Static friction
    *Answer: B*

58. **Which type of friction is the smallest?**
    A) Sliding
    B) Static
    C) Rolling
    D) Fluid
    *Answer: C (This is why wheels are used).*

59. **The ratio of limiting friction ($f$) to normal reaction ($R$ or $N$) is called:**

60. **The Coefficient of Friction ($\mu$) has unit:**
    A) Newton
