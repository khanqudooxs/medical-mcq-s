# Topic 09: Optics - Light, Mirrors & Lenses (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Nature of Light and Reflection

1. **Light is an ______ wave that can travel through a vacuum.**
   A) Mechanical
   B) Electromagnetic
   C) Matter
   D) Acoustic
   *Answer: B*

2. **The speed of light in a vacuum ($c$) is approximately:**
   A) $3 \times 10^5 m/s$
   B) $3 \times 10^8 m/s$ (300,000 km/s)
   C) $1100 m/s$
   D) $331 m/s$
   *Answer: B*

3. **Nothing in the universe can travel faster than the speed of light in a vacuum.**
   A) True
   B) False
   *Answer: A*

4. **The bouncing back of light from a polished surface is:**
   A) Refraction
   B) Reflection
   C) Diffraction
   D) Dispersion
   *Answer: B*

5. **According to the Law of Reflection, the Angle of Incidence ($i$) is always ______ the Angle of Reflection ($r$).**
   A) Greater than
   B) Equal to ($\angle i = \angle r$)
   C) Less than
   *Answer: B*

6. **The incident ray, the reflected ray, and the normal all lie in the ______ .**
   A) Same plane
   B) Different planes
   C) Circle
   *Answer: A*

7. **Reflection from a smooth, mirror-like surface is ______ reflection.**
   A) Diffuse
   B) Specular (Regular)
   C) Random
   *Answer: B*

8. **Reflection from a rough surface (like paper) that scatters light in many directions is:**
   A) Regular reflection
   B) Diffuse Reflection
   C) Total internal reflection
   *Answer: B*

9. **An image that can be formed on a screen is a ______ image.**
   A) Virtual
   B) Real
   C) Imaginary
   *Answer: B*

10. **An image that cannot be formed on a screen (like the one in a plane mirror) is a ______ image.**
    A) Real
    B) Virtual
    C) Solid
    *Answer: B*

    B) Away from

    A) Medium, Vacuum

    A) 1.0

    A) Polarizing angle

34. **When the angle of incidence exceeds the critical angle, all light is reflected back into the dense medium. This is:**
    A) Partial reflection

    A) Light must go from dense to rare

36. **Optical Fibers work on the principle of ______ .**
    A) Total Internal Reflection
    B) Refraction only
    C) Scattering

37. **The 'Mirage' (optical illusion in deserts) is a result of ______ .**
    A) Reflection from sand

38. **Sparkling of diamonds and the working of 'Endoscopes' are based on TIR.**
