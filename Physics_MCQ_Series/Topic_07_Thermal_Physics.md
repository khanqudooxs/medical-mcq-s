## Part 1: Temperature and Heat Basics

## Part 5: Gas Laws

    A) $V \propto T$

    A) Halved

    A) $V \propto 1/P$
    B) $V \propto T$ (Volume is proportional to absolute temperature)
    C) $PV = \text{Constant}$

    A) $P = VRT$

## Part 6: Laws of Thermodynamics

65. **The 'Zeroth Law of Thermodynamics' helps define the concept of:**
    A) Work
    B) Temperature
    C) Energy
    D) Entropy
    *Answer: B*

66. **The 'First Law of Thermodynamics' is a statement of the Law of Conservation of ______ .**
    A) Mass
    B) Energy ($\Delta Q = \Delta U + \Delta W$)
    C) Momentum
    *Answer: B*

67. **According to the First Law, heat added to a system ($\Delta Q$) increases its ______ ($U$) and does ______ ($W$).**
    A) Speed, Friction
    B) Internal Energy, External Work
    C) Mass, Weight

68. **An 'Isothermal' process is one that occurs at constant ______ .**
    A) Pressure
    B) Temperature
    C) Volume
    D) Heat
    *Answer: B*

69. **An 'Adiabatic' process is one in which ______ enters or leaves the system.**
    A) No Work
    B) No Heat ($\Delta Q = 0$)
    C) No Mass
    *Answer: B*

70. **An 'Isobaric' process occurs at constant ______ .**
    A) Temperature
    B) Pressure
    C) Volume
    *Answer: B*

71. **An 'Isochoric' (or Isovolumetric) process occurs at constant ______ .**
    A) Heat
    B) Volume
    C) Work
    *Answer: B*

72. **The 'Second Law of Thermodynamics' implies that heat cannot spontaneously flow from ______ to ______ .**
    A) Hot, Cold
    B) Cold, Hot
    C) High pressure, Low pressure
    *Answer: B*

73. **'Entropy' is a measure of the ______ of a system.**
    A) Energy
    B) Disorder or Randomness
    C) Cleanliness
    *Answer: B*

74. **According to the Second Law, the entropy of the universe is always ______ .**
    A) Decreasing
    B) Increasing
    C) Constant
    *Answer: B*

75. **A heat engine converts ______ energy into ______ energy.**
    A) Mechanical, Thermal
    B) Thermal (Heat), Mechanical (Work)
    C) Light, Power
    *Answer: B*

76. **A device that transfers heat from a cold body to a hot body by doing work is a:**
    A) Heater
    B) Refrigerator/Heat Pump
    C) Boiler
    D) Engine
    *Answer: B*

77. **Is it possible to build a 100% efficient heat engine?**
    A) Yes
    B) No (Carnot's theorem implies limits based on temperatures)
