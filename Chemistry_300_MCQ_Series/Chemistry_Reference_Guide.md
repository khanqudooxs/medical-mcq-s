## 4. Fundamental Constants and Units
*   **Avogadro's Number ($N_A$):** $6.022 \times 10^{23}$ particles/mol
*   **Ideal Gas Constant ($R$):** $0.0821 \text{ L}\cdot\text{atm}/\text{mol}\cdot\text{K}$ (or $8.314 \text{ J}/\text{mol}\cdot\text{K}$)
*   **Standard Temperature and Pressure (STP):** $0^\circ\text{C}$ ($273.15 \text{ K}$) and $1 \text{ atm}$ ($101.325 \text{ kPa}$)
*   **Molar Volume at STP:** $22.414 \text{ Liters}$
*   **Atomic Mass Unit (amu):** $1.66 \times 10^{-24} \text{ grams}$
*   **Speed of Light ($c$):** $3.00 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$
*   **Planck's Constant ($h$):** $6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J}\cdot\text{s}$

## 5. Important Formulas
*   **Mole Calculation:** $n = \frac{\text{Mass}}{\text{Molar Mass}}$
*   **Density:** $d = \frac{m}{V}$
*   **Ideal Gas Law:** $PV = nRT$
*   **Molarity:** $M = \frac{\text{Moles of solute}}{\text{Liters of solution}}$
*   **Molality:** $m = \frac{\text{Moles of solute}}{\text{kg of solvent}}$
*   **pH Calculation:** $\text{pH} = -\log[H^+]$
*   **Dilution Equation:** $M_1V_1 = M_2V_2$
*   **Percentage Yield:** $\frac{\text{Actual Yield}}{\text{Theoretical Yield}} \times 100\%$
