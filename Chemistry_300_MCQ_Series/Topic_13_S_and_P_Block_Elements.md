# Topic 13: s and p Block Elements (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: s-Block Elements (Groups 1 & 2)

1. **The s-block elements consist of which groups of the periodic table?**
   A) 1 and 2
   B) 13 to 18
   C) 3 to 12
   D) 17 and 18
   *Answer: A*

2. **Group 1 elements (excluding Hydrogen) are known as:**
   A) Alkaline earth metals
   B) Alkali Metals
   C) Transition metals
   ) Halogens
   *Answer: B*

3. **Group 2 elements are known as:**
   A) Alkali metals
   B) Alkaline Earth Metals
   C) Noble gases
   D) Metalloids
   *Answer: B*

4. **Which is the most reactive metal in Group 1?**
   A) Lithium
   B) Sodium
   C) Cesium (or Francium)
   D) Potassium
   *Answer: C*

5. **Why are Alkali metals stored under kerosene oil?**
   A) To make them shiny
   B) Because they react violently with oxygen and moisture in the air
   C) To prevent them from melting
   D) To increase their weight
   *Answer: B*

6. **Alkali metals have how many valence electrons?**
   A) 1
   B) 2
   C) 8
   D) 7
   *Answer: A*

7. **Alkaline earth metals have how many valence electrons?**
   A) 1
   B) 2
   C) 3
   D) 4
   *Answer: B*

8. **Which s-block element is essential for bone and teeth health?**
   A) Sodium
   B) Calcium
   C) Potassium
   D) Magnesium
   *Answer: B*

9. **Magnesium is a central component of which biological molecule?**
   A) Hemoglobin
   B) Chlorophyll
   C) Insulin

10. **The 'Flame Test' color for Sodium is:**
    A) Crimson red

11. **The flame test color for Potassium is:**
    A) Apple green

12. **Which is the lightest metal?**
    A) Sodium
    B) Aluminum
