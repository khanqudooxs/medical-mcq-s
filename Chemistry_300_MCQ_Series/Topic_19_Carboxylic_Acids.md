# Topic 19: Carboxylic Acids & Derivatives (150 MCQs Series)
## Part 1: Structure and Nomenclature

1. **Carboxylic acids are organic compounds containing the ______ functional group.**
   A) Carbonyl ($>C=O$)
   B) Hydroxyl ($-OH$)
   C) Carboxyl ($-COOH$)
   D) Amino ($-NH_2$)
   *Answer: C*

2. **The 'Carboxyl' group is a combination of which two groups?**
   A) Carbonyl and Hydroxyl
   B) Amino and Methyl
   C) Ether and Ester
   D) Nitro and Halogen
   *Answer: A*

3. **The general formula for aliphatic carboxylic acids is:**
   A) $R-CHO$
   B) $R-COOH$
   C) $R-CO-R$
   D) $R-O-R$
   *Answer: B*

4. **Which is the first member of the Carboxylic Acid series?**
   A) Acetic acid
   B) Formic Acid (Methanoic Acid, $HCOOH$)
   C) Benzoic acid
   D) Citric acid
   *Answer: B*

5. **Formic acid was first obtained by the distillation of:**
   A) Grapes
   B) Red Ants (Latin 'formica' means ant)
   C) Wood
   D) Vinegar
   *Answer: B*

6. **The second member of the series, $CH_3COOH$, is commonly known as:**
   A) Formic acid
   B) Acetic Acid (Ethanoic acid)
   C) Propionic acid
   D) Butyric acid
   *Answer: B*

7. **Acetic acid is the main component of ______ (4-8% solution).**
   A) Milk
   B) Vinegar
   C) Juice
   D) Wine only
   *Answer: B*

8. **Higher fatty acids (like Palmitic or Stearic acid) are used in making:**
   A) Glass
   B) Soaps and detergents
   C) Metals
   D) Salt
   *Answer: B*

9. **Suffix used for naming carboxylic acids in IUPAC is:**
   A) -al
   B) -ol
   C) -oic acid (e.g., Ethanoic acid)
   D) -one
   *Answer: C*

10. **The IUPAC name for $CH_3CH_2COOH$ is:**
    A) Ethanoic acid
    B) Propanoic acid
    C) Butanoic acid
    D) Methanoic acid
    *Answer: B*

11. **Dicarboxylic acids contain how many $-COOH$ groups?**
    A) 1
    B) 2 (e.g., Oxalic acid)
    C) 3
    D) 0
    *Answer: B*

12. **Oxalic acid ($HOOC-COOH$) is found in:**
    A) Lemons
    B) Tomatoes and Spinach
    C) Ants
    D) Milk
    *Answer: B*

13. **Benzene ring attached directly to a $-COOH$ group is:**
    A) Acetic acid
    B) Benzoic Acid ($C_6H_5COOH$)
    C) Salicylic acid
    D) Phenol
    *Answer: B*

14. **Carboxylic acids have ______ boiling points than alcohols of similar mass because they ______ .**
    A) Lower; don't bond
    B) Higher; form stable dimers through stronger hydrogen bonding
    C) Same; are isomers
    *Answer: B*

15. **Lower carboxylic acids (C1 to C4) are ______ in water.**
    A) Insoluble
    B) Highly soluble (due to hydrogen bonding)
    C) Oily liquids
    *Answer: B*

## Part 2: Acidity and Preparation

16. **Are carboxylic acids acidic?**
    A) No (they are neutral)
    B) Yes (they release $H^+$ ions in water)
    *Answer: B*

17. **Which is a stronger acid: Carboxylic Acid or Alcohol?**
    A) Alcohol
    B) Carboxylic Acid (due to resonance stabilization of the carboxylate ion)
    *Answer: B*

18. **Which is a stronger acid: Carboxylic Acid or Phenol?**
    A) Phenol
    B) Carboxylic Acid (generally)
    *Answer: B*

19. **How do Carboxylic Acids react with Sodium Bicarbonate ($NaHCO_3$)?**
    A) No reaction
    B) They react to produce $CO_2$ gas with effervescence (a test for $-COOH$ group)
    C) They explode
    *Answer: B*

20. **Oxidation of Primary Alcohols or Aldehydes with $KMnO_4/H^+$ produces:**
    A) Alkanes
    B) Carboxylic Acids
    C) Ketones
    D) Ethers
    *Answer: B*

21. **Hydrolysis of 'Nitriles' ($R-CN$) in acid or base produces:**
    A) Amines
    B) Carboxylic Acids
    C) Alcohols
    D) Nitrates
    *Answer: B*

22. **Reaction of Grignard Reagent ($RMgX$) with Carbon Dioxide ($CO_2$) followed by hydrolysis yields:**
    A) Alcohol
    B) Carboxylic Acid
    C) Alkane
    *Answer: B*

23. **Hydrolysis of Fats and Oils (Esters of fatty acids and glycerol) produces:**
    A) Sugars
    B) Fatty Acids and Glycerol
    C) Proteins
    *Answer: B*

24. **Which acid is found in sour milk and muscles after exercise?**
    A) Acetic
    B) Lactic acid
    C) Citric
    D) Malic
    *Answer: B*

25. **The presence of electron-withdrawing groups (like $-Cl, -F$) ______ the acidity of carboxylic acids.**
    A) Increases
    B) Decreases
    C) Has no effect
    *Answer: A*

26. **Which is a stronger acid: Acetic acid or Trichloroacetic acid?**
    A) Acetic acid
    B) Trichloroacetic acid (due to pull of three $Cl$ atoms)
    *Answer: B*

27. **Reduction of carboxylic acids with $LiAlH_4$ produces:**
    A) Aldehydes
    B) Primary Alcohols ($R-CH_2OH$)
    C) Alkanes
    D) Ketones
    *Answer: B*

28. **Reaction between a Carboxylic acid and an Alcohol in the presence of acid catalyst is:**
    A) Saponification
    B) Esterification
    C) Nitration
    D) Fermentation
    *Answer: B*

29. **Heating a carboxylic acid with $P_2O_5$ (a dehydrating agent) produces a/an:**
    A) Ester
    B) Acid Anhydride
    C) Acid Halide
    D) Amide
    *Answer: B*

30. **Reaction of carboxylic acid with $PCl_5$ or $SOCl_2$ produces a/an:**
    A) Amide
    B) Acid Chloride (Acyl chloride, $R-COCl$)
    C) Ester
    *Answer: B*

## Part 3: Acid Derivatives (Esters, Amides, etc.)

    *Answer: B*

    A) Pungent smell

36. **Urea is a diamide of which acid?**

37. **The general formula for an 'Acid Chloride' is:**

38. **Acid chlorides are highly reactive and react with water to give ______ and ______ .**

39. **The general formula for an 'Acid Anhydride' is:**
    A) $R-COOH$
    B) $(RCO)_2O$
    C) $R-O-R$

40. **'Aspirin' is an ester of Salicylic acid and ______ .**

    B) Acetic acid (specifically Acetic anhydride)

41. **Smallest dicarboxylic acid is:**
    A) Malonic acid
    B) Oxalic acid
    C) Succinic acid

42. **Vitamin C is ______ acid.**
    A) Citric
    B) Ascorbic
    C) Lactic
    *Answer: B*

43. **Tartaric acid is found in:**
    A) Milk
    B) Grapes and Tamarind
    C) Apples
    *Answer: B*

44. **Citric acid is found in:**
    A) Vinegar
    B) Lemons and Citrus fruits
    C) Protein
    *Answer: B*

45. **Is benzoic acid a solid at room temperature?**
    A) Yes (white crystalline solid)
    B) No

## Part 4: Amino Acids and Nitrogen Compounds

46. **Organic compounds containing both an amino group ($-NH_2$) and a carboxyl group ($-COOH$) are:**
    A) Fatty acids
    B) Amino Acids
    C) Sugars
    *Answer: B*

47. **Amino acids are the building blocks of:**
    A) Carbohydrates
    B) Proteins
    C) Fats
    D) Vitamins
    *Answer: B*

48. **In an 'Alpha-amino acid', the amino group is attached to the carbon ______ to the carboxyl group.**
    A) Adjacent ($\alpha$-carbon)
    B) Furthest from
    C) Inside
    *Answer: A*

49. **The bond that links amino acids together in a protein is the:**
    A) Ionic bond
    B) Peptide Bond (or Peptide linkage)
    C) Metallic bond
    D) Hydrogen bond
    *Answer: B*

50. **A peptide bond is chemically a/an:**
    A) Ester group
    B) Amide group ($-CO-NH-$)
    C) Ether group
    *Answer: B*

51. **Amino acids exist as dipolar ions called:**
    A) Cations
    B) Zwitterions (having both $+$ and $-$ charges)

52. **The simplest amino acid is:**
    A) Alanine
