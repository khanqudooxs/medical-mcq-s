# Nursing AI Question Bank - Set 2
## Subjects: International Standards (NCLEX-USA, EU-Germany, Pakistan-PNC, China)

### Priority & Leadership (NCLEX / International Style)
1. **(USA/Global)** A nurse is assigned 4 patients. Which patient should the nurse see FIRST?
   A) A patient waiting for discharge instructions.
   B) A patient with a post-operative pain score of 6/10.
   C) A patient with a new onset of shortness of breath.
   D) A patient requesting a glass of water.
   *Answer: C (Airway/Breathing is always priority 1)*

2. **(International)** The "ABC" rule in emergency triage stands for:
   A) Airway, Breathing, Circulation
   B) Assessment, Blood, Care
   C) Alert, Breathing, Cardio
   D) Airway, Bones, Capillaries
   *Answer: A*

3. **(Germany/EU)** In Germany, the concept of "Pflegedokumentation" (Nursing Documentation) is legally important because:
   A) It is for research only.
   B) "If it wasn't documented, it wasn't done."
   C) It is only for the billing department.
   D) Patients are not allowed to read it.
   *Answer: B*

4. **(Pakistan/PNC)** In a community health setting in Pakistan, the primary focus of the LHV (Lady Health Visitor) is:
   A) Surgery
   B) Maternal and Child Health
   C) Specialized ICU care
   D) Pharmacy management
   *Answer: B*

5. **(Global)** To prevent medication errors, how many 'Checkpoints' should the nurse perform before giving a drug?
   A) 1
   B) 2
   C) 3 (Check when picking, preparing, and giving)
   D) 5
   *Answer: C*

### Pharmacology (International Dosage)
6. **(Math)** The order is for 0.25 mg. You have 250 mcg tablets. How many tablets do you give?
   A) 1 tablet
   B) 2 tablets
   C) 0.5 tablets
   D) 10 tablets
   *Answer: A (0.25mg = 250mcg)*

7. **(Safety)** Which drug is a 'High-Alert' medication globally and requires a double-nurse check?
   A) Multivitamins
   B) Insulin
   C) Saline Nasal Spray
   D) Paracetamol syrup
   *Answer: B*

8. **(Route)** Which route of medication avoids "First-Pass Metabolism" by the liver?
   A) Oral (PO)
   B) Sublingual (SL)
   C) Rectal (partially)
   D) IV (Fully)
   *Answer: D (IV) and B (SL). Use D for 100% bypass.*

9. **(China/Asia Standard)** In many Asian hospitals, the use of Traditional Medicine alongside Western medicine is called:
   A) Alternative Care
   B) Integrated Medicine
   C) Illegal Care
   D) Placebo Care
   *Answer: B*

10. **(Pharmacology)** A patient is on Warfarin (Blood thinner). What is the international standardized lab test used to monitor its effectiveness?
    A) WBC
    B) Hemoglobin
    C) INR (International Normalized Ratio)
    D) Blood Glucose
    *Answer: C*
